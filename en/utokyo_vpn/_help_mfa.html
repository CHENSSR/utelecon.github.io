Troubleshooting guide: When the error message “You don’t have access to this” appears There is a possibility that you have not completed the Information Security Education. If that is the case, you cannot use UTokyo VPN. Please contact the Security Team of the Information Environment Division at the headquarters, `jouhousecurity.adm __at__ gs.mail.u-tokyo.ac.jp` (change `__at__` to @), with your UTokyo Account user name.
If the issue is still not resolved, please consult the Technical Support Desk.
