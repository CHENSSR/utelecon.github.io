Troubleshooting guide
  • During the LINE linking procedure after clicking “Connect LINE”, you are taken back to the previous page, or the page turns white: This error means there is a problem with the linkage between ITC-LMS and LINE. Please try again from the beginning. For more details, please refer to the ITC-LMS announcement "LINE linkage registration fails (in Japanese)".
  • The "Linked e-mail address" in the "Forwarding e-mail address" field is not displayed: This might be due to your email address registered in UTAS not being reflected in the ITC-LMS yet. It takes a day for it to be reflected; please try again on the next day.
If the issue is still not resolved, please consult the Technical Support Desk.
